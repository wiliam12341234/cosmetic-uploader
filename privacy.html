1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SoutheastAsiaCosmeticUploader ("we", "our", or "the Service") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our automated product upload service for TikTok Shop.

Transparency Commitment: We believe in being transparent about our data practices. This policy details what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Authentication Information

When you connect your TikTok Shop account:

  • OAuth Tokens: Access tokens and refresh tokens provided by TikTok API
  • Account Information: Basic shop information (shop ID, name, region)
  • Permissions: Granted API scopes for product management

2.2 Product Data

When processing products for upload:

  • Product Information: Titles, descriptions, prices, images from 1688 platform
  • Processing Data: Translation results, optimized pricing, categorization
  • Upload Status: Success/failure status of product uploads

2.3 Usage Data

To improve our service:

  • Log Data: Timestamps, actions performed, error messages
  • Performance Metrics: Processing times, success rates
  • Feature Usage: Which features are used most frequently

3. How We Use Your Information

Information Type Purpose Legal Basis
OAuth Tokens Authenticate with TikTok API to manage products Contractual necessity
Product Data Process and upload products to TikTok Shop Contractual necessity
Usage Data Improve service performance and fix issues Legitimate interest
Error Logs Diagnose and resolve technical problems Legitimate interest

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Duration

  • OAuth Tokens: Stored until you revoke access or delete your account
  • Product Data: Temporary processing only, deleted after upload completion
  • Log Data: Retained for 30 days for troubleshooting

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access to production data
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability scanning
  • Data Minimization: Only collect data necessary for service operation

Important: While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. We recommend you use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication on your TikTok account.
